"AgentOS" and the AgentOS name and logo are trademarks of Curie Engineering
(the "Marks"). This policy explains how you may and may not use the Marks. It is
separate from the [Apache License 2.0](LICENSE) that covers the source code: the
license grants broad rights to use, modify, and redistribute the code, but it
does **not** grant any rights to use the Marks. Trademark rights protect users
from confusion about what is official and what is not, and they exist even for
open-source projects.

## What you can do without asking

- **Refer to the project by name.** Use "AgentOS" in truthful, descriptive
  statements about the project, such as "compatible with AgentOS," "built on
  AgentOS," or "a plugin for AgentOS."
- **Redistribute the software unmodified** under the Apache License 2.0,
  keeping the AgentOS name that identifies it.
- **Write about AgentOS** in articles, tutorials, talks, and documentation.
- **State that your product integrates with or supports AgentOS**, as long as
  the statement is accurate and does not imply endorsement or an official
  relationship.

## What requires our permission

- Using "AgentOS" (or a confusingly similar name) as the name of your own
  product, service, company, or a modified distribution.
- Using the Marks in a way that suggests your project, product, or company is
  **official, endorsed by, affiliated with, or sponsored by** Curie Engineering
  when it is not.
- Using the AgentOS logo in a modified form, or using the Marks in a domain
  name, social handle, or app-store listing for your own offering.
- Merchandise or any commercial use of the Marks beyond truthful reference.

## Modified versions

If you distribute a modified version of AgentOS, you must not present it as the
official AgentOS or imply that Curie Engineering endorses it. Give your fork a
distinct name, and you may accurately state that it is "based on AgentOS" or "a
fork of AgentOS."

## General guidance

- Do not alter or abbreviate the Marks in ways that create a new mark.
- Do not incorporate the Marks into your own logos or trademarks.
- When in doubt, use the Marks to point to this project, not to brand your own.

## Questions

If you are unsure whether your use is acceptable, or you would like permission
for a use that requires it, open an issue at
https://github.com/curie-eng/agentos or contact Curie Engineering. We are happy
to work with the community and generally grant reasonable requests.
